About

I'm Youngchan Kim, a Ph.D. student in the Dept. of Visual Computing and AI at MPI for Informatics, advised by Prof. Christian Theobalt. My research interests focus on generative human model and relighting and facial animation and motion synthesis at the intersection of Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, and Machine Learning.

Previously, I completed my Master's degree at POSTECH Computer Graphics Lab under the supervision of Prof. Seung-Hwan Baek, working on high-dimensional light properties [NeSpoF, spDataset] and light-matter interaction modeling [hpBRDF]. Before pursuing graduate studies, I received my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics & Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Yonsei University.
